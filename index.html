Requirement Analysis

We begin by understanding your product needs — including styles, fabrics, quantities, timelines, target prices, and technical specifications. This ensures alignment from day one.

Vendor Selection & Costing

We match your requirements with the right factory from our trusted network. We negotiate competitive pricing while ensuring compliance, quality, and capacity.

Sampling & Product Development

We develop and deliver pre-production samples based on your tech packs or inspirations. Revisions and improvements are made until you’re fully satisfied.
