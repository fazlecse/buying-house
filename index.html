Requirement Analysis

We begin by understanding product needs- Includes Styling, Yarn types, Fabrication, Embelishments, Types of Trims & Accessories, quantities, timelines, target prices & technical specifications. This ensures alignment from day one.

Vendor Selection & Costing

We match your requirements with the right factory from our trusted network. We negotiate competitive pricing while ensuring compliance, quality, and capacity.
